# Code of Conduct

Be civil. Be specific. Assume good faith.

This project welcomes contributions from anyone willing to engage
constructively. What we ask in return:

- **Feedback targets the work, not the person.** "This has a race condition
  on line 42" is fine. "You're careless" is not.
- **Assume the other person is acting in good faith** until you have
  evidence otherwise.
- **Stay on topic.** Issues, PRs, and discussions are for Tesseron's
  technical work. They're not the venue for unrelated politics or
  personal grievances.
- **Respect time zones and review cadence.** Maintainers have day jobs.
  Nudging after a week is fine; demanding responses isn't.
- **Harassment, discrimination, and personal attacks are grounds for
  immediate removal** from the project's issues, PRs, and discussions.
  Repeat offences result in a permanent ban.

Enforcement decisions are made by the project maintainers and are final.
If you think a decision was applied unfairly, email
`kenny.vaneetvelde@gmail.com` with the subject prefix
`[tesseron-coc]` and the case will be re-reviewed.

This document is intentionally short. Its spirit is captured in the first
three words: be civil, be specific, assume good faith.
